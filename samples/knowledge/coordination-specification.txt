SECTION 01 10 00 GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

Coordinate new partition locations with the reflected ceiling plan, lighting layout, fire alarm
devices, sprinkler heads, and mechanical diffusers before construction. Report unresolved
clearance conflicts to the design team before procurement or installation.

SECTION 08 71 00 DOOR HARDWARE

Provide lever hardware sets at the revised conference-room doors. Confirm handing, frame
preparation, access-control power, and hardware schedule references against the current issued
revision. Where the drawing and hardware schedule disagree, preserve both records and request
written clarification before ordering.

SECTION 26 05 00 ELECTRICAL COORDINATION

Relocated partitions require review of receptacles, switches, data outlets, and life-safety
devices. Field dimensions and detected drawing changes are decision-support evidence only and
must be verified against the source documents.
