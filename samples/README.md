# Samples

Only synthetic or openly licensed blueprint fixtures belong here. Built-in results must be clearly
labelled as samples and paired with their exact source revisions and engine metadata.

Customer uploads, temporary artifacts, generated model caches, and private drawings must never be
committed.

`schematic/` contains a synthetic control-loop baseline/candidate pair, expected normalized regions,
and a supporting design-note document for the `engineering_schematic` profile. Mechanical drawings
and packaging artwork remain possible future profiles; they are not supported in v0.2.
